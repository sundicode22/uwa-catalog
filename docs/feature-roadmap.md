# Feature Roadmap

Potential functionalities to add to the platform, organized by area. Based on the current codebase (stores, products, orders, customers, billing, storefronts, discounts, i18n).

---

## Storefront & Shopping

- Custom domain per store
- Wishlist / favorites
- Product reviews & ratings
- Related / recommended products
- Product bundles & combos
- Pre-orders & back-in-stock alerts
- Gift cards
- Abandoned cart recovery
- Guest checkout vs. saved customer accounts
- Storefront dark mode / theme picker
- Custom CSS / advanced branding
- More catalog layout templates
- Product comparison
- Size guide / rich product tabs
- Multi-language storefront content (not just UI strings)

## Products & Inventory

- Bulk import/export (CSV, Excel)
- Duplicate product
- Bulk edit (price, stock, category)
- Product tags & collections
- Scheduled publish / unpublish
- Low-stock email/SMS alerts
- Inventory history & audit trail
- Barcode / SKU scanner support
- Supplier management
- Cost price & profit margin tracking
- Product drafts & version history

## Orders & Fulfillment

- Order notes & internal comments
- Order timeline / activity log
- Partial fulfillment & split shipments
- Shipping zones & rate tables
- Delivery time slots
- Printable invoices & packing slips
- Refund workflow (full UI, not just transactions)
- Order status SMS/email to customers
- Driver / delivery assignment
- Pickup location management (multiple points)

## Payments & Billing

- More payment gateways (PayPal, Mobile Money, etc.)
- Split payments / deposits
- Tax rules (VAT, regional rates)
- Multi-currency checkout
- Subscription / recurring products
- Payout dashboard for merchants
- Invoice generation for platform billing

## Marketing & Growth

- Email campaigns (promotions, newsletters)
- SMS marketing
- Referral / affiliate program
- Loyalty points & rewards
- Flash sales & countdown timers
- Pop-ups & announcement bars on storefront
- Coupon auto-apply rules (min spend, first order, etc.)
- Google / Meta product feed sync
- UTM & conversion tracking
- SEO tools per store (meta editor, sitemap preview)

## Customers & CRM

- Customer segments (VIP, inactive, high spenders)
- Customer order history on storefront
- Customer tags & notes
- Birthday / anniversary offers
- Repeat purchase reminders
- Customer export & GDPR data request tools

## Dashboard & Analytics

- Sales reports (daily/weekly/monthly)
- Best-selling products report
- Traffic & visitor analytics
- Conversion funnel (view → cart → order)
- Revenue by category / payment method
- Export reports as PDF/CSV
- Real-time dashboard notifications
- Goal tracking (monthly sales targets)

## Team & Permissions

- Multi-user team members with roles (admin, staff, viewer)
- Activity log (who changed what)
- Two-factor authentication (2FA)
- API keys for merchant integrations
- Webhooks for order/product events

## Integrations

- WhatsApp Business API (automated messages)
- Instagram / TikTok shop sync
- Accounting export (QuickBooks, Xero)
- Zapier / Make automation
- Slack / Discord order alerts
- Cloud print for receipts

## Platform & Admin

- Super-admin panel (manage all merchants)
- Feature flags per plan
- Usage limits dashboard
- Onboarding checklist & guided setup
- In-app help / knowledge base
- Live chat support widget
- Mobile app (merchant + customer)
- Public API documentation
- White-label / reseller mode

## Legal & Trust

- Privacy policy & terms generator per store
- Cookie consent banner
- Order dispute handling
- Fraud detection (duplicate orders, suspicious IPs)

## Quality of Life

- Global dashboard search (products, orders, customers)
- Keyboard shortcuts
- Undo for destructive actions
- Auto-save on forms
- Offline-friendly storefront (PWA)
- Push notifications for new orders
